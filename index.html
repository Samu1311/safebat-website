The Silent Failure

Leaking batteries are
quietly destroying your home.

When batteries reach end-of-life or are stressed, corrosive electrolytes escape. They don't just ruin remotes—they pose health hazards and generate toxic electronic waste.

Corrosive Damage

Leaked chemicals damage circuit boards and terminals, making expensive devices instantly unusable.

Health Hazards

Electrolytes can irritate skin and respiratory systems. In severe cases, leaked fluids become highly flammable.

Environmental Impact

Improper disposal contaminates soil and water. Billions of batteries are sold yearly with zero built-in protection.

The Technology

The SafeBat Protector Capsule.

Unlike ordinary storage cases, the SafeBat Protector lives *inside* your device. It encloses single-use batteries in a chemically active environment that waits for a leak to happen—then neutralises it instantly.

  • Neutralises corrosive chemicals instantly
  • Reduces fire and explosion risks
  • Universal low-cost installation
