The problem

Leaking batteries quietly damage devices and the environment.

As disposable batteries age or are stressed, their electrolytes can leak, corroding circuit boards, damaging terminals and releasing corrosive chemicals into homes, soil and water.

Household impact
Billions

of AA/AAA batteries sold yearly for remotes, toys and appliances – many devices end up ruined by leaks.

Health & safety

Leaked electrolytes can irritate skin, eyes and lungs and, in some chemistries, are flammable.

Environmental harm

Improperly disposed leaking batteries contaminate soil and water and undermine circular-economy goals.

The SafeBat Protector

A simple capsule that neutralises leaks inside your devices.

SafeBat Protector is a low-cost, eco-friendly capsule that encloses AA/AAA batteries inside devices. A reactive liner neutralises leaked electrolyte, preventing corrosion and capturing harmful chemicals so they cannot escape into the environment.

Protects devices

Prevents corrosive leaks from damaging terminals, circuit boards and internal electronics.

Protects families

Neutralises hazardous chemicals that can irritate skin, eyes and lungs while reducing fire and explosion risks.

Protects the planet

Captures leaked electrolyte so it cannot contaminate soil and water, supporting sustainable battery use.

How it works

Chemistry and engineering, simplified.

A recyclable outer casing holds the batteries, while a proprietary reactive liner safely interacts with any leaked electrolyte. The reaction neutralises corrosive compounds, keeping them inside the capsule.

Built by a family team of electronics, chemistry, mechanical and software engineers across Venezuela, Spain and Denmark, SafeBat blends lab-grade science with everyday household use.
