Product

SafeBat Protector: the capsule that neutralises battery leaks.

A low-cost, eco-friendly capsule for AA/AAA batteries that sits inside devices, captures leaked electrolyte and neutralises it before it can cause damage.

Reactive liner

A proprietary inner layer reacts with leaked electrolyte, neutralising corrosive chemicals and stopping them from reaching device components.

Recyclable casing

The outer casing uses recyclable materials, designed with sustainability and manufacturing scale in mind.

Universal fit

Initial sizes support AA/AAA batteries for remotes, toys and small appliances, with more formats planned.

Simple to use

Fit, load, forget.

SafeBat is designed to drop into existing battery compartments. Consumers install it once, add batteries and then use the device as usual.

  1. Step 1
    Place capsule in compartment

    Insert the SafeBat Protector into the battery compartment, aligning it with the existing contacts.

  2. Step 2
    Insert batteries as usual

    Load AA/AAA batteries into the capsule and close the device as you normally would.

  3. Step 3
    Protected over time

    If electrolyte leaks, the liner captures and neutralises it, helping keep contacts and electronics safe.

Roadmap

From AA/AAA capsules to a family of safety products.

SafeBat’s long-term vision includes new sizes, multi-battery packs and digital tools that support safer, smarter battery use.

Additional sizes

Future variations for C, D and 9V batteries expand protection to more appliances and equipment.

Smart monitoring

A future app could help households track battery status, replacement cycles and proper disposal.

Partnerships

Collaborations with battery brands, electronics manufacturers and recycling programs can scale SafeBat’s impact.

SafeBat aims to become a standard part of battery-powered devices, embedding safety and sustainability directly into how we power our homes.
