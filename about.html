Origin Story

Founded by a family of engineers.
Driven by a shared problem.

SafeBat wasn't born in a corporate boardroom. It started with a ruined remote control and a conversation between family members across Venezuela, Spain, and Denmark.

Our Mission

To protect everyday devices and the environment by creating science-driven solutions that neutralise battery leaks, extend product life, and make sustainable electronic habits accessible for all.

Our Vision

A future where every household uses safe, durable, and environmentally friendly battery technologies. We aim to embed protection into every battery-powered product worldwide.
