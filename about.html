About SafeBat

A family-built startup redefining battery safety.

SafeBat was founded by a family of engineers and scientists across Venezuela, Spain and Denmark who experienced the same problem: battery leaks quietly destroying their devices and harming the environment.

Mission

We protect everyday devices and the environment by creating science-driven solutions that neutralise battery leaks, extend product life and make sustainable electronic habits accessible for all.

Vision

We envision a future where every household uses safe, durable and environmentally friendly battery technologies. SafeBat aims to embed protection and sustainability into every battery-powered product worldwide.

Core values

  • Safety – neutralise leaks to protect devices and people.
  • Sustainability – prevent contamination and support proper recycling.
  • Innovation – apply cutting-edge science to everyday problems.
  • Simplicity – create low-cost, easy-to-use solutions.

Brand personality

Scientific, eco-conscious, innovative, family-rooted and protective.

SafeBat combines chemistry and engineering with a warm, authentic family narrative. We emphasise safety and credibility without fear-mongering, and explain complex topics in accessible language.

Founders’ story

From household frustration to a global battery-safety mission.

SafeBat started when four family members kept finding ruined remote controls, toys and devices caused by leaking batteries. Each brought a different skill set to the same problem.

Electronics & chemistry

An electronic engineer in Venezuela mapped how leaks corrode circuitry, while a chemist in Spain developed ways to neutralise leaked electrolytes safely.

Software & mechanics

A software engineer in Denmark supports digital tools, website and future monitoring apps, while a mechanical engineer in Spain designs the capsule’s physical structure and manufacturability.

A multi-disciplinary, multi-national team

Together, the family built prototypes and validated that SafeBat’s liner neutralises leaks while remaining eco-friendly. That mix of skills shapes everything from product design to future partnerships.
