Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the SafeBat Protector capsule, compatibility, and safety.

Product & Compatibility

Does the capsule make the battery too thick?

↓
No. The SafeBat Protector is engineered with ultra-thin, high-tensile materials. It is designed to fit into standard AA and AAA battery compartments found in remote controls, toys, and appliances without forcing the springs.

Is it reusable?

↓
If no leak occurs, yes! You can reuse the SafeBat Protector indefinitely with new batteries. If a battery leaks inside the capsule, the reactive liner neutralises the acid and changes colour. In this case, you should dispose of the capsule and the battery safely.

What battery sizes are supported?

↓
We are currently launching with AA and AAA sizes, which cover over 80% of household device needs. We have C, D, and 9V versions in our R&D roadmap.

Safety & Environment

How does the neutralization work?

↓
Our proprietary liner contains a dry chemical agent that activates instantly upon contact with alkaline electrolyte (potassium hydroxide). It triggers a safe chemical reaction that lowers the pH to a non-corrosive level and solidifies the liquid.

Is the capsule recyclable?

↓
Yes. The outer shell is made from 100% recycled high-impact polymer. If the capsule has neutralized a leak, it contains the waste safely, preventing it from leaching into soil if disposed of in standard waste, though we always recommend battery recycling centers.
