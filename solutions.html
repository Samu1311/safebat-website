Solutions

One simple capsule. Several meaningful protections.

SafeBat solutions are built around protecting devices, households and the environment from the hidden consequences of leaking batteries.

Protect your devices

Stop corrosion before it happens.

The SafeBat Protector neutralises leaks inside battery compartments, preventing corrosion on terminals, contacts and circuit boards.

  • • Ideal for remotes, game controllers, toys and small appliances.
  • • Reduces replacement costs and frustration.

Protect your family

Keep harmful chemicals under control.

By capturing and neutralising leaked electrolyte, SafeBat helps reduce exposure to corrosive compounds that can irritate skin, eyes and lungs.

  • • Extra reassurance in children’s toys and household devices.
  • • Supports safer battery use in everyday homes.

Protect the planet

Reduce contamination and waste.

Neutralised electrolytes are contained inside the capsule, helping to prevent contamination of soil and water when devices and batteries are disposed of.

  • • Aligns with circular-economy and ESG principles.
  • • Makes sustainable habits more accessible to households.

Who SafeBat is for

Designed for households, retailers and manufacturers.

SafeBat starts with everyday households but is built to scale through retail shelves and OEM partnerships.

Households & DIY users

Eco-conscious parents and home electronics enthusiasts protect devices while modelling sustainable habits for their families.

  • • Use SafeBat in remotes, toys, flashlights and controllers.
  • • Reduce device replacement and clean-up from leaks.

Retailers & distributors

Electronics stores, supermarkets and online marketplaces can offer SafeBat as an add-on that increases basket size and sustainability credentials.

  • • Place SafeBat beside batteries and small electronics.
  • • Use eco messaging to attract sustainability-focused shoppers.

OEM manufacturers & partners

Appliance and toy manufacturers can integrate SafeBat capsules into new products or co-package them with batteries out of the box.

  • • Enhance product safety and sustainability stories.
  • • Create new co-branded offerings with battery manufacturers.
