Comprehensive Protection

One simple capsule.
Three critical layers of safety.

SafeBat isn't just a case; it's a reactive chemical shield. We designed it to address the three major failures of modern batteries: device corrosion, health risks, and environmental contamination.

Device Protection

Stops corrosion before it touches your terminals. Ideal for expensive remotes, game controllers, and vintage electronics.

  • Prevents circuit board damage
  • Extends device lifespan

Family Safety

Neutralises toxic electrolytes that can burn skin or eyes. Essential for households with children’s toys and everyday gadgets.

  • Reduces chemical exposure risks
  • Minimizes fire/explosion hazards

Planet Impact

Prevents soil and water contamination. SafeBat capsules ensure that when a battery dies, its chemicals die with it.

  • Supports circular economy
  • Responsible disposal made easy
