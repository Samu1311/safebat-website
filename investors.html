Investor overview

A first-mover in reactive battery leak neutralisation.

SafeBat is building a new product category at the intersection of battery safety, household electronics and sustainability.

Market & opportunity

A household-scale problem with global relevance.

Billions of AA/AAA batteries are sold yearly for remotes, toys, controllers, flashlights and small appliances. Many devices are damaged by leaks, while leaking batteries contaminate soil and water and pose health risks.

Problem

Leaked electrolyte corrodes terminals and boards, harms health and reduces battery efficiency.

Gap

Existing consumer products focus on storage and transport. None neutralise leaks inside devices.

Opportunity

A simple, low-cost accessory that aligns with eco-trends and circular-economy goals.

Unique selling proposition

Reactive capsules that neutralise leaks, not just hold batteries.

Unlike ordinary battery cases, the SafeBat Protector uses a proprietary chemical reaction to neutralise leaked electrolyte inside devices, preventing corrosion and environmental contamination.

Technology

Reactive liner neutralises corrosive compounds while remaining safe and eco-friendly.

Economics

Low manufacturing cost, mass-market price point and strong add-on potential at retail.

Moat

Patentability of chemistry, first-mover advantage and potential brand equity with early adopters and partners.

Roadmap & focus

From household pilot to platform for safe battery use.

  1. Phase 1
    Prototype validation & early adopters

    Complete lab testing, refine materials, and run pilots with eco-conscious households and DIY users.

  2. Phase 2
    Retail launch & OEM pilots

    Enter selected retail channels and explore co-packaging with appliance and toy manufacturers.

  3. Phase 3
    Expanded portfolio & digital layer

    Add new capsule sizes, multi-battery formats and a smart app layer for monitoring and education.
