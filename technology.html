Seamless Integration

Fit. Load. Forget.

We designed SafeBat to be invisible in your daily life—until you need it. Installation takes seconds and requires no tools.

01

Insert Capsule

Place the empty SafeBat Protector into your device's battery compartment like a sleeve.

02

Load Battery

Slide your standard AA/AAA battery inside the capsule. It snaps securely into place.

03

Total Protection

Use your device. If the battery leaks, the capsule neutralises it instantly, keeping your device clean.
